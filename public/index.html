Five Steps

The Morning Ritual

Each step takes five minutes. Each one is designed to be gentle, repeatable, and achievable — even on the most chaotic mornings.

01
🏃
Move

Wake Up Your Body

Gently shift your nervous system from sleepy and tense to energized and steady. Stretches, twists, marching, a balance game, a shake-out — gentle and repeatable.

5 minutes
02
📚
Unpack

Name What You're Carrying

Name the emotions you're carrying so they stop running unconsciously in the background. Write or say one sentence, rate the intensity 0–10, add a kind response.

5 minutes
03
🌡
Breathe

Find Your Calm

Guided breathing for calm, focus, and flexibility. Sit comfortably — inhale 6 counts, exhale 4 counts, 8–12 rounds. Sun breaths for the little ones.

5 minutes
04
✨
Gratitude

Connect Through Appreciation

Build connection through appreciation and belonging. Gratitude reframes your perspective and deepens the bonds you already have — as a family, as a person.

5 minutes
05
🎯
Intention

Set Your Direction

Set behavioral goals with if-then planning. Decide how you want to show up today — for yourself, your children, your work. Small decisions, big results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MindBodyRitual?

MindBodyRitual is a 25-minute morning routine made up of five 5-minute rituals — Move, Unpack, Breathe, Gratitude, and Intention. It is designed for caregivers and children ages 3 and up, and is available as a free app and e-book.

What ages is this designed for?

The ritual is designed for caregivers and children ages 3 and up. The kid-friendly language and gentle activities are accessible for young children while remaining meaningful and grounding for adults.

What if we don’t have a full 25 minutes?

No problem. The guiding principle is “better than before, not perfect.” On busy mornings, do the first 2 minutes of any step and move on. Even a small dose of the ritual sets a better tone for the whole day.

Is the app free?

Yes, the app is free to download on iOS and Android. You can also get the complete e-book version by signing up with your email in the section above.
