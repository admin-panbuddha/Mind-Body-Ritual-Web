Frequently Asked Questions

What is MindBodyRitual?

MindBodyRitual is a wellness e-commerce platform that combines curated products with guided daily rituals and a gamified reward system. We help you build a consistent wellness practice through beautiful tools and real motivation.

How does the gamification work?

When you log daily rituals, you earn streak points, collect ritual stones (crystals categorized by Mind, Body, and Ritual), and progress along a journey map. Real milestones unlock real rewards — discounts, free samples, and exclusive products.

Do you ship to Canada?

Yes! We're based in Canada and offer free shipping on orders over $75 CAD. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within Canada.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all products. If you're not happy, reach out and we'll make it right — exchange, store credit, or full refund.

Can I cancel my subscription?

If you subscribe to a ritual box, you can cancel anytime from your account dashboard. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
